Steam Press

  • Steam Press is a modern way of ironing clothes. It is very different from the traditional irons that are used at homes. It irons the clothes in a much faster and crisper way. In this process, the iron is filled with water. At a high temperature, the water gets converted into steam and that steam is used to press the clothes. The use of steam removes the wrinkles and crushes on clothes in a very easy manner.
  • Steam Ironing is a very effective way of ironing clothes because it makes the clothes look like they have just been bought. It is said that steam ironing kills bacteria, pollen and other germs which are present on the clothes but are not visible to naked eyes. This helps in keeping the clothes clean and healthy.
